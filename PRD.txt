## 1. Product Overview & Vision

The **Financial Health Assessment Platform** empowers SMEs with **AI-driven financial clarity**, enabling informed decision-making without requiring financial expertise.
The product acts as a **virtual CFO for SMEs**, translating complex financial data into **simple, actionable insights**.

**Vision:**
To democratize financial intelligence for SMEs and improve their access to credit, operational efficiency, and long-term sustainability.

---

## 2. Problem Breakdown & User Pain Points

### Key Challenges Faced by SMEs

* Limited financial literacy among business owners
* Fragmented financial data across spreadsheets, GST filings, and bank statements
* Poor cash-flow visibility and delayed risk detection
* Difficulty accessing appropriate financial products
* Manual bookkeeping and compliance errors

---

## 3. Target Users & Personas

### Primary Persona: Non-Finance SME Owner

* Owns or operates a small business
* Relies on accountants for compliance, not insights
* Needs **plain-language answers**, not accounting jargon

### Secondary Personas

* Startup founders seeking investor readiness
* SME lenders evaluating creditworthiness
* Accountants and financial advisors

---

## 4. User Journeys & Key Use Cases

1. **Upload financial documents** or connect bank/GST data
2. **Select industry type** (Retail, Agriculture, Manufacturing, etc.)
3. View:

   * Financial Health Score
   * Cash runway
   * Risk indicators
4. Ask questions in natural language:

   * ‚ÄúWhy is my cash balance declining?‚Äù
   * ‚ÄúCan I afford to expand next quarter?‚Äù
5. Receive:

   * Actionable recommendations
   * Product suggestions
   * Investor-ready reports

---

## 5. Functional Requirements

### Core Features

* Financial data ingestion (CSV, XLSX, PDF)
* Automated categorization of income and expenses
* Financial ratio analysis
* Creditworthiness assessment
* Risk flagging (liquidity, leverage, compliance)

### Advanced Features

* Bookkeeping assistance
* GST compliance validation
* Forecasting & projections
* Working capital optimization
* Industry benchmarking
* Multilingual output

---

## 6. Financial Health Score & Hybrid AI Logic

### Hybrid Computation Model

* **Python (pandas):**

  * Liquidity ratios
  * Profitability metrics
  * Cash runway
  * Working capital cycle
  * Composite Financial Health Score
* **LLM (GPT-5 / Claude):**

  * Explains score drivers
  * Highlights risks
  * Provides recommendations
  * Answers natural language queries

This ensures **accuracy, explainability, and AI safety**.

---

## 7. Non-Functional Requirements

### Security & Compliance

* AES-256 encryption for data at rest
* TLS 1.3 for data in transit
* Role-based access control
* Audit logs for financial actions

### Performance & Scalability

* Handle concurrent SME uploads
* Modular architecture for adding industries and APIs

---

## 8. System Architecture (High-Level)

### Layered Architecture

* **Ingestion Layer:**
  Pandas-based pipelines for CSV/XLSX/PDF ingestion
* **Intelligence Layer:**
  Financial computation + LLM orchestration
* **Application Layer:**
  React/Angular frontend
* **Data Layer:**
  PostgreSQL (encrypted)
* **Security Layer:**
  Encryption, access control, compliance
